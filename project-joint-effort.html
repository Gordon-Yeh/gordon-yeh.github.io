Joint Effort

JointEffort is a visual feedback tool that tracks, speeds recovery and prevents further injury for physiotherapy patients trying to recover from injuries using at home exercises.

My Work

I was responsible for developing the frontend (web app). The challenge was communicating with the designers efficiently to complete within the short time frame (~10 hours). It was a great opportunity to work in a multidisciplinary team from medical experts, to UI designers, to fellow engineers.
