Attentiv Medical

IV infiltration is the most common complication related to IV therapy, it affects 24% of adults and 70% of NICU babies who receive IV infusions. Our team developed an innovative solution that embeds a sensor into the IV catheters to detect the exact moment when the IV fails. This improves the current standard of care which mainly consists of visual detection.
