Queue Up!

NWhacks 2020 Winner of best use of standard library

Queue Up is an online portal for restaurants and customers to help them navigate the confusing and often frustrating process of managing or waiting in queues. It is a web app that is built with React and uses Autocode as its back end.

I was responsible for the design (figma) and development of the front-end. My biggest takeaway was scoping requirements given a short time constraint (less than 24 hours), and improving on staying calm and productive under the stress of time and sleep deprivation.
